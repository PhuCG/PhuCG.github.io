Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2023

Welcome to Cory Ebook, the ebook reading app that allows you to purchase and unlock the books you desire using gems. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

Information Collection

When you use Cory Ebook, we collect basic information about you, including:

  • Account: When you create an account, we collect the information you provide us, such as your email.
  • Purchase Information: We track the books you have purchased and unlocked with gems in the app, as well as the books you have downloaded for offline reading.

Information Usage

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To manage your account: We use your email to create an account for you and allow you to log in. If you choose to delete your account, we will delete all personal information related to your account.
  • To provide services to you: We use your purchase information to ensure that you can access and read the books you have purchased and unlocked, as well as the books you have downloaded for offline reading.
  • To improve our app: We may use your information to understand how users use our app and to improve our app.

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or as described in this privacy policy.

Privacy Policy Modification

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes.
