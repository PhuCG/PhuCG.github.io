Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2023

Welcome to Cory Ebook, the ebook reading app that enriches your reading experience with a wealth of literary content. This Privacy Policy details our commitment to protecting your personal information and outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and under what circumstances it may be shared.

Information Collection

In the course of using Cory Ebook, we collect information that helps us provide you with our service, including:

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we gather the information you provide such as your name and email address.
  • Reading Preferences and History: We document your reading preferences and activity to personalize your experience and suggest content that aligns with your interests.

Information Usage

We use the information we collect for several purposes:

  • Account Management: Your email address is utilized for account creation, management, and to facilitate your engagement with our services. If you decide to delete your account, we will also remove all personal information associated with it.
  • Personalized Experience: Your reading history and preferences help us to customize your reading experience and offer content recommendations suited to your tastes.
  • App Enhancement: We may analyze usage patterns to understand how our users interact with the app, which helps us to improve the application and develop new features that enhance user satisfaction.

Information Sharing

Your personal information is not shared with any third parties, except to comply with legal obligations or as detailed in this privacy policy.

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email.
